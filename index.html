About the shows

The 2025 Broadway house will be showing three outstanding musicals. All three of them will leave you excited to come back for your next musical.

musical list

Musical lists

come experience these amazing shows for yourself

  1. Musical 1: Wicked
    • A story of friendship, magic, and the truth behind the witches of Oz.
    • Elphaba and Glinda couldn’t be more different—but their bond changes everything.
    • Get ready for powerful music, stunning visuals, and a twist on a story you thought you knew.
  2. Musical 2: Les Miserables
    • A powerful tale of redemption, sacrifice, and hope in the face of injustice.
    • Follow Jean Valjean as he tries to rebuild his life while being hunted by the relentless Inspector Javert.
    • With unforgettable music and heart-wrenching stories, this show will leave you breathless.
  3. Musical 3: Phantom of the Opera
    • A haunting love story set beneath the grand Paris Opera House.
    • A mysterious musical genius falls for a young soprano, drawing her into his dark world.
    • With sweeping music and unforgettable visuals, it’s a romantic thriller you won’t want to miss.
